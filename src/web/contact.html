Youth Generation

Youth Generation is a student-led non-profit group that aims to spread awareness of global issues and inspire the young generation to become global citizens and future change makers. We are composed of members from different schools, backgrounds, and countries, such as China, Korea, Singapore, Malaysia, and the United States.
